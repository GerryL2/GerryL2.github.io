What's the reason for Zoos?

Though we all love zoos for the main reason of being able to see animals when they are not in the wild
but it is always important to remember the actual reasons for Zoos and why they are some important. It may
thought that zoos are keeping animals in captivity and not allowing them to be able to their lives fully in
natural habitat but zoos are doing do opposite, often being a place where animals can rehabilitate and grow up
somewhere safer from where they would usually be. For example with animals out in the wild often being in certain
states where they're species may becoming extinct, a zoo plays a big part in researching them, keeping safe, and
helping their species to mate more and prolong their species. ALso it is frowned upon by some that people are going
to the zoos and thought that they may be making the animals feel bad but this too is often not the case. The zoo also
helps people and their families that come to educate themselves on animals and the money that is generated from them
is most often used to help raise money for conservation efforts, make the zoo areas better for the animals, and money more
ways that benefit the animals.
